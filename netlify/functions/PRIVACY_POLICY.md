# Privacy Policy for Movie Hub

**Last Updated: May 1, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Movie Hub ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Movie Hub (the "App").

**Movie Hub is a movie discovery app that helps you find, track, and organize your favorite films.** We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Watchlist & Favorites**: Movies you save to your watchlist or favorites list
- **Ratings**: Star ratings (1-5) you assign to movies
- **Watch History**: Movies you've watched and your viewing progress
- **Preferences**: Your genre preferences during onboarding

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Information**: Device model, operating system version, and app version
- **Usage Data**: App interactions, screen views, and feature usage patterns
- **Crash Reports**: Anonymous crash data to help us improve app stability

### 2.3 Information NOT Collected
We do **NOT** collect:
- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data (GPS or IP-based)
- Contact list or calendar data
- Photos, media, or files from your device
- Advertising ID or tracking data

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- **Personalize your experience**: Show relevant movie recommendations
- **Track your watch history**: Help you remember movies you've watched
- **Maintain your watchlist and favorites**: Keep your saved movies organized
- **Improve our service**: Analyze usage patterns to enhance app features
- **Fix crashes**: Diagnose and resolve technical issues

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Local Storage
**All your personal data (watchlist, favorites, ratings, watch history) is stored locally on your device only.** We do not upload this data to any external servers.

### 4.2 App Data
Your data remains on your device until you:
- Clear app data from device settings
- Uninstall the application
- Use the "Clear All Data" option within the app

### 4.3 Security Measures
We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data:
- Local encryption for sensitive preferences
- Secure API communications (HTTPS)
- Regular security updates

## 5. Third-Party Services

### 5.1 YouTube API Services
Movie Hub uses **YouTube's official embedding API** to stream movie content. All video playback is served directly from YouTube's servers. We do not host, store, or redistribute any video content.

**YouTube's data handling is governed by:**
- [YouTube Terms of Service](https://www.youtube.com/t/terms)
- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [YouTube API Services Terms](https://developers.google.com/youtube/terms/api-services-terms-of-service)

### 5.2 Movie Data API
We use external APIs to fetch movie metadata (titles, descriptions, cast, genres, thumbnails). This data is publicly available movie information only.

### 5.3 No Tracking or Analytics Services
**Movie Hub does not use any analytics, crash reporting, or tracking services.** We do not integrate with:
- Google Analytics
- Firebase Analytics
- Crashlytics
- Any advertising SDKs
- Any third-party tracking pixels

## 6. Content Copyright & Ownership

### 6.1 Video Content
All video content displayed in Movie Hub is embedded from **official YouTube distributors** using YouTube's official embedding API. We do not:
- Host or store any video files on our servers
- Download or cache YouTube videos
- Bypass YouTube's advertising or content restrictions
- Claim ownership of any video content

### 6.2 Movie Metadata
Movie information (posters, descriptions, cast, etc.) is sourced from publicly available movie databases and remains the property of their respective copyright holders.

### 6.3 YouTube Embedding Compliance
We fully comply with YouTube's Terms of Service regarding video embedding. If a video owner disables embedding, the video will not play within our app.

## 7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

### 7.1 Delete Your Data
You can delete all your locally stored data at any time:
- **Within the app**: Go to Settings → Data Management → Clear All Data
- **Device settings**: Clear app data from Android settings
- **Uninstall**: Removing the app deletes all locally stored data

### 7.2 Opt-Out
Since we do not collect personal data, there is no data to opt out of. All your data remains local to your device.

### 7.3 Export Your Data
Your data is stored locally. You can view all your saved movies within the app's Watchlist and Favorites sections.

## 8. Children's Privacy

Movie Hub does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age. The app contains only movie-related content that is generally suitable for all ages, but parental guidance is recommended.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us, and we will take appropriate action.

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be:
- Posted within the app
- Reflected by the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
- Communicated via app updates

We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

## 10. Data Retention

Your data is retained locally on your device for as long as you use the app. When you:
- **Clear data**: Data is immediately deleted from your device
- **Uninstall the app**: All locally stored data is removed

## 11. International Data Transfers

Since all user data remains on your local device, no international data transfers occur. Movie data is fetched from publicly available APIs and YouTube's global content delivery network.
